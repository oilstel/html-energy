About
Marie Otsuka (https://motsuka.com) is a designer working around tools and methods for making. She designed and developed the solar powered version of the Low-Tech Magazine website (https://solar.lowtechmagazine.com).
This episode was originally recorded in New York on December 30, 2019, not too long after Marie returned from Barcelona, where the solar-powered server of Low-Tech Magazine lives.
