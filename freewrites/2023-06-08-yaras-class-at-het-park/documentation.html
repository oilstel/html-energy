Yara Veloso's Wdka students freewrite with HTML Energy

June 8th, 2023 – Het Park, Rotterdam

❇️

prompt: participants were instructed to handwrite an html page, drawing inspiration from their environment in the park. they swapped their pages with a classmate, who interpreted their peer's html as if they were a web browser.









































our gratitude to Yara Veloso for providing us with the opportunity to conduct this workshop, and for her html energy
