The Energy of HTML

  • Ultimately, any attempt to describe HTML energy must come from what people actually experience at writing HTML. These testimonials remind us that writing HTML can only be truly understood through direct participation.

  • HTML stands for Peace, Love, Unity and Respect. These four ideals are often touted as the elements that make writing HTML more positive and life affirming than other kinds of markup languages.

  • At the heart of HTML energy there's the pulse of something intangible; a positive unifying groove, a extraordinary feeling, an Element that transcends it's attributes.

  • Anyone who has ever been involved with the internet knows how easy it is to become philisophical and idealistic about what it all means. For some, raving points towards a positive vision for the future of humankind.

  • Website builders are like shamans forging portals to other realms. Science and technology give us the tools. HTML energy explores the synergy between what you see and what you get.

  • Whether you believe that writing HTML is a spiritual revolution that is going to change the world or just a chance to make a website, the one thing we can all agree on is that writing HTML is FUN!

  • Though writing HTML is a spontaneous of the moment activity, the ability to completely let go of our mental and emotional constraints often depends on an underlying sense of security and safety. Everything is interconnected; love and respect involves being aware, and taking care of ourselves and each other.

  • Writing HTML is now a part of our culture and history. The things we do in our code editors are not new or totally unique. Nor does writing HTML happen in complete isolation from other existing cultural forces.

  • Websites are like Temporary Autonomous Zones (TAZs) - separate, selfgoverning events that take place between the cracks of society's fabric. But sometimes the web0 community is forced to deal with the politics of a world in turmoil.

  • Online connections; information is exchanged, a loose community evolves. Technology fosters communication: Interacting on the internet helps bring us together.

  • As we open our minds and transcend our limits, we can explore links to things beyond our browsers where new ideas and information await our discovery.
