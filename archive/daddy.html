what we've accomplished so far...

freewrites

through irl and online freewrites we've built a community of people excited a more personal, handmade, practical, slower world wide web. we've organized freewrites in parks and online. our irl freewrites connect us with each other and also with nature.

 community

our twitter & email newsletter brings our www community together for HTML freewrites and generally gets our people excited about HTML.

 podcast

we have been talking with artists, educators, and developers on our podcast about how they use HTML and where they want to see the web go in the next 10-20 years. this is another way we've been making HTML more accessible to more people.

why

we believe that the best of the internet is yet to come. although we aren't really about web3 we believe that web3 people share many of the same values and ambitions as web0 people. we want to see a new less corporate web but we believe that it's already possible we just have to unlock it. we believe there is much to be explored when it comes to making websites. we also believe that through education and tooling we can bring things like (HTML & CSS) to more people. As educators and students we also strongly believe that people need to be inspired to pursue knowledge. In the past we've created directories of inspiring websites. We hope to do more of this type of work.
